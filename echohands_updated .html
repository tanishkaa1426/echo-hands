GETTING STARTED

How to Use EchoHands

Get up and running in minutes. Here's your step-by-step guide.

1

Create Your Account

Click "Get Started" on the top right and fill in your name, email and set a password. You can also sign up using Google. Once registered, you'll land on your personal dashboard.

2

Set Up Your AI Avatar

From the Dashboard, click "Edit Avatar". Choose an emoji or upload a photo, set your display name, and pick your preferred sign language (ASL, BSL, ISL, etc.). Your avatar will represent you across all communication sessions.

3

Choose a Communication Mode

EchoHands adapts to your context. Select a Mode from the Dashboard:

  • Hospital Mode – Medical phrases & doctor communication cards
  • Classroom Mode – Student-focused vocabulary & captions
  • Travel Mode – Multi-language phrases for navigation
  • Restaurant Mode – Food ordering & allergy communication
  • Emergency Mode – Instant SOS & alert tools
4

Communicate in Real-Time

Use the Communication Hub to: type a message and have it spoken aloud (Text-to-Speech), speak and get instant captions (Speech-to-Text), or let the AI avatar perform sign language. All modes support two-way real-time communication.

5

Learn Sign Language

Head to the Learning Hub to start gamified sign language courses. Complete modules, earn badges and track progress on your dashboard. The AI Avatar Teacher demonstrates every sign with accuracy.

6

Join the Community

Visit the Community Hub to connect with peers, join student groups, find mentors or discuss accessibility topics in moderated forums. All spaces are safe and inclusive.

7

Set Up Emergency Contacts

Go to the Emergency Hub and add at least 2 emergency contacts. In a crisis, press the SOS button — your contacts will receive your location and a help alert instantly.
